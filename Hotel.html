Victoria Angkor Hotel

Room Types:
52 Victoria Superior Rooms, 68 Victoria Deluxe Rooms and 10 Spacious and themed suites including: Japonaise, Pierre Loti, and Maharaj Suite

Room Features: Private balcony or garden, hair dryer, Cable TV, Minibar, IDD Direct Dial Telephone, Air-Conditioning, Bathroom with Bathtub & Shower Room, In room Safety Box, Coffee making facilities.

Facilities: Outdoor Jacuzzi, Spa and Massage, Library, Swimming Pool, Business Center, Boutique, Tour arrangements, Pool table, Conference Room, Internet centre.

Food and Beverage: Le Bistro De Siem Reap, L’Escale, Le Jardin de l’Apsara, Bar L’Explorateur.
